fSTR2 works in a tier system. Compare your result to the table below. To move up or down a tier you will need to add STR

The most simple way to determine whether your need 2 or 3 STR at a time to increase 1 fSTR2 point is to compare your total STR to your Job Level

If total STR is higher than job level, it's safest to assume 3 STR at a time is required. If STR is below job level, it's safest to assume 2 STR at a time is required.
